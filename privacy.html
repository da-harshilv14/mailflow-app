Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 29, 2026

The short version

  • MailFlow runs entirely on your own machine — no servers, no cloud.
  • Your emails are never stored, transmitted, or shared with anyone.
  • We only request the minimum Gmail permission needed to filter your inbox.
  • You can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.
  • We collect no personal data and run no analytics.

1. Who we are

MailFlow is a personal, open-source Gmail automation tool. It is not a company or commercial product. The source code is publicly available on GitHub and can be audited by anyone.

This privacy policy describes how MailFlow handles information when you use it to filter your Gmail inbox.

2. What data MailFlow accesses

When you authorise MailFlow with your Google account, it accesses the following information from your Gmail mailbox:

  • Email headers — the From address, Subject line, and Date of each email.
  • Email snippets — a short preview of the email body (provided by the Gmail API), used to match keyword-based filter rules.
  • Label information — the list of labels currently applied to your emails and mailbox.

MailFlow does not access the full body text of emails, attachments, contacts, calendar data, Google Drive, or any other Google service.

3. How MailFlow uses your data

All data accessed from your Gmail account is used solely to evaluate filter rules locally on your machine. Specifically:

  • Email headers and snippets are read in memory and compared against your configured rules.
  • Matching emails are labelled and archived within Gmail using the Gmail API.
  • Non-matching emails are checked for importance signals and flagged accordingly.

All processing happens locally. MailFlow runs as a script on your own computer. No email content, metadata, or results are sent to any external server, database, or third-party service.

4. Data storage

MailFlow stores exactly one file on your machine:

  • token.json — your OAuth 2.0 access and refresh tokens, saved locally so you do not have to log in on every run. This file is created in your working directory and never transmitted anywhere.

No email content, no metadata, and no usage logs are written to disk at any point.

MailFlow never

  • Sends or forwards emails on your behalf
  • Permanently deletes any emails
  • Stores email content in any database or file
  • Transmits any data to external servers
  • Shares any information with third parties
  • Runs analytics or tracks usage
  • Accesses your Google account outside of Gmail

5. Google OAuth and permissions

MailFlow requests access to your Gmail account using Google's OAuth 2.0 authorisation flow. The only scope requested is:

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.modify

This scope allows MailFlow to read emails and modify labels (add, remove, archive). It does not allow sending emails or permanently deleting them.

Your credentials are handled entirely by Google's official OAuth libraries. MailFlow never sees or stores your Google account password.

6. Third-party services

MailFlow uses the Google Gmail API to interact with your mailbox. By using MailFlow, your use of Gmail remains subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Google's Terms of Service.

MailFlow itself does not integrate with, transmit data to, or depend on any other third-party service.

7. How to revoke access

You can revoke MailFlow's access to your Gmail account at any time:

  • Go to myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Find MailFlow in the list of connected apps
  • Click Remove access

You can also delete the token.json file from your machine to remove locally stored credentials.

8. Children's privacy

MailFlow is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Because MailFlow collects no data and has no user accounts, we have no way to notify users directly — please check this page periodically if you wish to stay informed.
